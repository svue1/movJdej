movJdej

Mov ntse dej, meaning "rice with water", is a recipe website aimed to become the the first of its kind. Its goal is to document and host a community supported, free Hmong recipe database online. It was developed as a Senior Capstone project at Macalester College based in Saint Paul, Minnesota in 2022. See the "about" page to learn more about how movJdej came to be!
